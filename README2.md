# 🧠 NeuroSync

### *AI-Powered Intelligence for Smarter Healthcare Decisions*

---

## 🚀 Overview

**NeuroSync** is a next-generation AI-driven healthcare assistant designed to analyze user symptoms, lifestyle patterns, and personal health data to deliver **structured insights, intelligent recommendations, and predictive health analysis**.

Unlike traditional symptom checkers, NeuroSync combines **AI intelligence with neuroscience-inspired design** to create a **smart health dashboard experience**.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

In today’s world, people struggle to interpret health symptoms accurately due to lack of accessible, personalized, and intelligent tools. Existing solutions are often generic, unstructured, or difficult to use.

NeuroSync solves this by providing **clear, structured, and personalized health insights** in a simple and interactive way.

---

* Preventive Tips

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Frontend:** React.js
* **Styling:** Tailwind CSS
* **Animations:** Framer Motion
* **3D/Background:** Three.js / Canvas
* **Charts:** Recharts / Chart.js
* **AI Integration:** LLM-based API
